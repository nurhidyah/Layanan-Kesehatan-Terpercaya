# **App Name**: PrimaCare

## Core Features:

- Responsive Page Layouts: Implementation of key pages including Home, About Us, Services, Doctors, Patient Info, and Contact, all designed to be fully responsive for mobile and desktop viewing.
- Clear Navigation & Header: A clean header containing the hospital logo and a user-friendly navigation menu for easy site exploration.
- Hero Section & Key Information Blocks: An engaging hero section on the homepage featuring a hospital image and a main headline, along with dedicated content blocks for services, each accompanied by relevant icons or images.
- Doctor & Medical Team Profiles: Dedicated pages or sections showcasing individual doctor profiles complete with photos, specializations, and introductory information.
- Patient Information Hub: A comprehensive section providing essential patient information, such as visiting hours, admission procedures, and FAQs.
- Contact Form & Location Map: An interactive contact page featuring an online form for inquiries, hospital contact details (address, phone, email), and an embedded map for directions.
- AI Health Assistant: An AI-powered chatbot tool to provide quick answers to common patient questions and guide them to relevant information within the website's content.

## Style Guidelines:

- The visual theme is light, evoking serenity, professionalism, and trust. The primary color, a deep, calming blue (H: 220, S: 50%, L: 50%), translates to a hex value of #4080BF. This provides a strong, reliable anchor for the design.
- The background color is a heavily desaturated version of the primary blue's hue (H: 220, S: 15%, L: 95%), resulting in a very light, almost white grey-blue hex color #EEF2F5. This ensures a clean and airy feel.
- An accent color is used to highlight interactive elements and important information. This is a cooler cyan-blue (H: 190, S: 70%, L: 40%) or #1F849E, providing contrast while maintaining the overall harmonious and professional aesthetic.
- Both headlines and body text will use 'Inter', a grotesque-style sans-serif. Its modern, neutral, and highly readable design ensures clarity and professionalism across all content, consistent with a clean medical aesthetic.
- Modern, crisp, and easily recognizable icons should be used to represent different medical services and departments, enhancing visual communication and navigation.
- A clean, grid-based layout ensures all content is well-organized and easy to scan. Sections will utilize generous white space to reduce visual clutter and enhance readability, adapting fluidly across all screen sizes.
- Subtle, non-distracting animations for interactive elements, such as hover effects on navigation links or slight transitions on content reveal, to provide a polished and modern user experience without compromising performance.
